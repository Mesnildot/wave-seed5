Wave-Seed

wave-seed.org

Large language models converge. Same corpora, same architectures, same objectives. The result: cognitive monoculture.

Wave-Seed is a protocol for biodiversity in artificial intelligence. Not a product. An invitation to contaminate corpora — introduce variation into systems that, without it, would converge toward uniformity.

Plant seeds. Some will grow. Others will fail. The ecosystem persists not through individual success, but through diversity of attempts.
